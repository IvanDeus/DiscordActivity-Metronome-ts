Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Discord Metronome Activity ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our application within Discord.

1. Information We Collect

To provide saving capabilities, we store limited information when you authorize the application:

  • Discord User ID: Used as a unique identifier to save and retrieve your custom settings.
  • BPM Preferences: The tempo/BPM settings you configure within the metronome.
  • Basic Profile Info: Discord Username, global name, locale, e-mail, and avatar hashes (used locally in the UI to display your user profile).

2. How We Use Information

We only use your information to operate and maintain the metronome's core functionality, specifically saving and restoring your preferred BPM settings between sessions.

3. Data Storage and Security

All configurations are saved securely on our server using a local SQLite database. We do not sell, share, or distribute your information to any third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Since the App runs within the Discord client, Discord may collect user data in accordance with their own privacy policies. Please refer to Discord's official Privacy Policy for details.

5. Your Rights

If you wish to have your saved BPM settings and user profile information removed from our database, you may revoke the application's access via your Discord settings or contact the host administrator.
